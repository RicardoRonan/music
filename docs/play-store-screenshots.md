# Play Store Screenshot Plan

Frame 1:
Title: Offline music, made simple
Subtitle: Play local MP3 files with a clean no-clutter player
Screen: Library screen with music loaded
Benefit: Communicates core value immediately for search traffic

Frame 2:
Title: Scan your device in seconds
Subtitle: Find music on your phone and start listening fast
Screen: Empty library state with "Scan device" CTA
Benefit: Reduces first-time confusion and improves install-to-play conversion

Frame 3:
Title: Powerful now playing controls
Subtitle: Seek, queue, shuffle, repeat, and like in one view
Screen: Now playing screen with transport controls visible
Benefit: Shows depth without complexity

Frame 4:
Title: Your local library, organized
Subtitle: Browse songs, albums, artists, playlists, and folders
Screen: Library categories and folder view toggle
Benefit: Proves it handles real local collections well

Frame 5:
Title: Find tracks instantly
Subtitle: Search songs, albums, artists, folders, or files
Screen: Search screen with filter chips and results
Benefit: Highlights speed and usability for large libraries

Frame 6:
Title: Favorites and recently played
Subtitle: Keep your best tracks one tap away
Screen: Library sections for liked songs + recently played
Benefit: Reinforces retention and daily-use behavior

Frame 7:
Title: Build playlists your way
Subtitle: Save queue and organize listening sessions
Screen: Playlist list and playlist detail view
Benefit: Shows long-term utility beyond basic playback

Frame 8:
Title: No ads. Just your music.
Subtitle: Lightweight offline player built for focused listening
Screen: Clean now playing or home view without overlays
Benefit: Strengthens brand promise and differentiates from ad-heavy apps

## Visual direction notes
- Use short headline text (3-6 words) with high contrast.
- Keep one main message per frame.
- Prefer real in-app screens over mockups.
- Keep consistent typography and spacing across all frames.
