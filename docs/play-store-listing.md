## Full description
Music is a simple offline music player for Android that plays your local MP3 and audio files with a clean, lightweight interface.

Built for listeners who want fast playback without clutter, accounts, or ads, Music helps you scan your device, import tracks, and enjoy your library offline in seconds.

### Why install Music
- Offline music player for local files
- Clean and modern UI focused on listening
- Fast local library scan and file import
- MP3 player controls with queue, repeat, and shuffle
- Favorites, playlists, and recently played
- Search songs, albums, artists, folders, and files

### Core features
- **Local music playback**: play MP3 and other local audio files stored on your phone
- **No ads listening experience**: focus on your music with a calm interface
- **Library organization**: browse by playlists, albums, artists, and folders
- **Now playing controls**: quick access to seek, queue, like, shuffle, and repeat
- **Helpful first-run setup**: clear guidance to import or scan music on first launch
- **Privacy-first approach**: your local library stays on your device

### Great for
- Users who want a local music player
- People searching for an offline music player
- Anyone looking for a simple MP3 player on Android
- Listeners who prefer a no ads music player experience

Music is continuously improved for better performance, stability, and smoother local playback on Android devices.
